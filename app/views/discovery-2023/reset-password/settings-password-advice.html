Reset your password

If you have forgotten your password you need to visit NHS login settings.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to NHS login settings.
  2. Enter the email address you used to create your NHS login account.
  3. Select reset your password.
  4. We will send you an email with a link to reset your password.
  5. Go back and enter your new password. If you need to start again, use the link in the email we sent you to finish setting up your email.
